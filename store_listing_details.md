### Full Description (max 4000 chars)
Welcome to **LetterBloom: Daily Word Garden**, a uniquely relaxing yet challenging word puzzle game! Combining the best of word searches with a beautiful hexagonal honeycomb grid, LetterBloom offers a fresh, daily-themed puzzle to keep your brain sharp and your garden growing.

**How to Play:**
- **Swipe & Spell:** Swipe across adjacent honeycomb tiles to form words.
- **Daily Themes:** Every day brings a new theme (Garden, Ocean, Storm, Cozy, Bakery, Citrus, Forest, Space). Find the specific goal words to complete today's garden!
- **Bonus Words:** Found a valid English word with 3 or more letters? Earn bonus points! ✨
- **Combo Multiplier:** Keep finding words back-to-back without missing to build up your x2 combo multiplier! ⚡
- **Blooming Tiles:** Tiles containing goal-word letters will bloom 🌷! They keep their letters and can be reused to find even more words.
- **Daily Streak:** Come back every day to maintain and grow your streak! 🔥

**Features:**
- A massive dictionary of over 100,000 common English words.
- Relaxing, beautiful aesthetics with animated pulsing selections, blooming petal decorations, and smooth gradient backgrounds.
- Fluid swipe selection with backtrack capabilities.
- Persistent stats to track your streaks, daily bests, and found words.

Download LetterBloom today and let your vocabulary bloom!
