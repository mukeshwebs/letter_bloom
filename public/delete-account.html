🌸 LetterBloom — Delete Your Account

Last updated: May 3, 2026

We respect your right to control your data. This page explains how to delete your LetterBloom account and all data associated with it.

1. What Gets Deleted

When you request account deletion, the following data is permanently removed from our systems:

  • Your account profile (display name, email address)
  • Game progress (scores, levels completed, daily streaks)
  • Achievements and badges earned
  • Found words history
  • Leaderboard entries linked to your account
  • Authentication credentials (Firebase Auth record)

2. Delete Directly From the App (Recommended)

  1. Open LetterBloom on your device.
  2. Make sure you are signed in.
  3. Tap the Profile / Settings icon.
  4. Scroll down and tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm the action when prompted.

Your account and all associated data will be removed immediately.

3. Request Deletion by Email

If you no longer have access to the app or prefer to request deletion manually, send us an email from the address linked to your LetterBloom account with the subject line Delete My Account.

📧 Email letterbloom.app@gmail.com

Please include:

  • The email address used to sign in
  • Your in-game display name (if known)

We will process your request and delete your data within 30 days and send a confirmation email once complete.

4. Data Retained After Deletion

For legal, security, and fraud-prevention reasons, we may retain a minimal set of non-identifying records (such as anonymized crash logs and aggregate analytics) that cannot be linked back to you. No personally identifiable information is kept after deletion.

5. Can I Recover My Account?

Account deletion is permanent and irreversible. Once deleted, your progress, scores, and achievements cannot be restored. You are welcome to create a new account at any time.

6. Questions?

If you have any questions about the deletion process, contact us at letterbloom.app@gmail.com.
